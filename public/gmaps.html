Communities Against Corona

A responsive application that provides a platform for people too seek help and provide help during the Covid19 crisis..

How To Use The Map:

Firstly a marker can be created to show that you are able to provide resources. Click on the plus icon on the Side Bar and click on the Add Event button. Here you can add information such as your name, phone number and addres. You are also able to specify a type or category for your maker and a short description. This short description should be used to tells us more information such as the time you are avaliable and other details

Furthermore, you can also edit your marker if you realize you have incorrect information or you want to delete a marker. Click on the edit icon to manage your markers. In here, you can view all your created markers. To update information, click on the marker you want to change. A form will be made visible and here you can update the fields as you see fit. To delete a marker, click on the marker and click on the Delete Marker found below the form and next to the Update button.

To log out, click on the settings button (gear icon) found on the bottom left corner and click on the Log Out button.
