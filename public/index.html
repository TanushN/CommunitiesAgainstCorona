We must take action when people around us need help. By providing resources, we are making sure that our family, friends, and neighbors are staying healthy during the Corona Virus quarantine.

How It Works

This responsive website that works on both PC and Mobile is designed to give you a platform where you can help others and provide them with resources. Say you have extra toilet paper. You can create a marker indicating that you're able to provide toilet-paper. The map will then show the marker to everyone and display your contact information for others needing help to reach out to you.
